💬 Using ARIA every day

Chat with your AI team

Pick an agent from the dropdown at the top of Chat — each one has its own job and expertise — and talk to it like a colleague. Attach files, paste screenshots, or press 🎤 to speak.

Type / at the start of a message for quick actions: /task makes a to-do, /remember saves a fact, /research looks something up online, /artifact drafts a document.

Give them real work

Tasks tracks to-dos (they can repeat weekly, monthly…). Assign a task to an agent and it gets done. Agent Hub hands out work; Workload shows who's busy. Agents raise alerts when something needs you.

Teach them your business

Upload price lists, processes, product info in Knowledge — agents use it automatically when they answer.

🛟 Good to know

I forgot my password
Staff: your administrator resets it in Settings → Team access, or sends you a fresh invite. Admins: on the sign-in screen click "Locked out? Use your recovery key" and use the key you saved at setup. Keys work once — generate a new one afterwards.
I typed the wrong password too many times
After 5 wrong tries ARIA makes you wait — 30 seconds at first, doubling each time (15 minutes max). This protects your account. Just wait and try again.
Does it work without internet?
Signing in and your saved work: yes, for up to 14 days offline. AI replies need a connection (they use the Anthropic service).
Is my data backed up?
ARIA saves a snapshot automatically once a day and keeps the 7 newest — find them via Settings → Data → Open backups folder. Before big changes, click Export backup for a manual copy.
Windows blocked the installer
Click More info → Run anyway on the blue screen. This appears because the app is new and not yet code-signed — it's expected.
My invite code "isn't valid"
Codes work once and expire after 7 days. Ask your administrator to click Copy join link on a fresh invite.
The AI isn't replying / shows an error
Check your internet, then check the API key in Settings → AI & API. The key must be active with credit at console.anthropic.com.
Is ARIA up to date?
It updates itself within a few hours of each release. To check right now: Settings → About → Check for updates now.
I want to start over completely
Settings → Data → Export backup first (just in case), then Wipe all data. ARIA restarts like a fresh install.
