ARIA

Setup & User Guide

ARIA is your AI business assistant — specialist agents that chat, complete assigned tasks, and keep your company knowledge. It runs on your own computer; your data stays with you.

1Install ARIA

  1. Download the installer with the button above and run it.
  2. Windows may show a blue "Windows protected your PC" screen the first time (the app isn't code-signed yet). Click More info → Run anyway.
  3. Follow the installer. ARIA opens when it finishes.

On first launch ARIA asks who you are: Administrator sets up a workspace for a team, Staff joins one that already exists. Working alone? Choose "It's just me on this PC — skip logins" and jump to Using ARIA.

2Set up as the administrator

Admin The administrator manages accounts, invites, company setup and API keys. Staff get the day-to-day app.

  1. On the first screen choose Administrator → Set up this workspace.
  2. Enter your company name, your name, email, and a password (8+ characters).
  3. ARIA shows your recovery key (looks like ARIA-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX). Copy it and store it somewhere safe outside ARIA — a password manager or a printed copy.
  4. Complete onboarding: paste your Anthropic API key (from console.anthropic.com → API keys). It's stored encrypted on your machine and only ever sent to Anthropic.
The recovery key is shown once. If every admin password is forgotten, this key is the only way back in. You can generate a new one any time in Settings → Team access.

3Set up the cloud workspace

Admin The cloud workspace lets your team sign in from their own computers with just an email and password — no file passing. It stores logins, membership and your shared company setup only; conversations and documents never leave each PC.

  1. Create a free project at supabase.com and run the schema — the full walkthrough is in supabase/README.md (about 10 minutes, one time only).
  2. In ARIA open Settings → Team access → Cloud workspace, paste the project URL and anon key, and click Connect.
  3. Create account — your cloud identity (can be the same email as your local login).
  4. Create workspace — you become its admin.
  5. Click ⬆ Publish company setup — this shares your agents, business profile and org chart with every member automatically.

To bring someone in: create an invite in the same section (choose staff or admin), then click Copy join link and send it to them however you like — message, email, sticky note. Invites are single-use and expire after 7 days.

4Join as a staff member

Staff You need the join link (or invite code) from your administrator.

  1. Download and install ARIA on your own computer.
  2. On the first screen choose Staff → Join with an invite code.
  3. Paste the join link (starts with ARIA-JOIN-…), enter your name and email, and choose your own password (8+ characters).
  4. You're in. Your company's agents and business profile arrive automatically within a few seconds.

From now on, sign in with just your email and password — on this PC or any other one with ARIA installed. Your conversations stay on your machine and are yours alone.

5Using ARIA day to day

💬 Chat

The heart of ARIA. Pick an agent from the dropdown at the top (each has its own role, personality and expertise) and talk to it like a colleague. Attach files, paste screenshots, or press 🎤 for voice. Type / at the start of a message for quick commands: /task creates a task, /remember saves a fact to company memory, /research runs web research, /artifact drafts a document.

🤖 Agents

Your AI team. Create or edit agents in the Agents view — give them instructions, expertise and an autonomy level. Agents can execute assigned tasks on their own, raise alerts when goals stall, and write weekly reports. The Agent Hub assigns work; Workload shows who's doing what.

☑️ Tasks & projects

Tasks tracks to-dos (with recurring schedules); Projects groups related conversations and artifacts. Assign a task to an agent and it gets done — with research when web research is enabled.

📚 Knowledge

Upload documents and notes the agents should know: price lists, processes, product specs. Agents reference them automatically in answers.

🏠 Dashboard

Your morning view: open tasks, unread alerts, agents at work, and recent conversations.

6What's shared, what's private

Shared with your team (via the cloud)Private to each computer
Agents and their instructionsConversations and chat history
Business profileKnowledge documents
Org chart / workspace structureTasks, projects, artifacts
Who belongs to the workspace (logins)Agent learnings from chats
The line is enforced in the software, not by policy. The published company setup physically cannot contain conversations, documents, tasks or passwords. If it's confidential, it stays on the PC where it was created.

ARIA also snapshots your data automatically once a day (7 newest kept). Find them via Settings → Data → Open backups folder, and make manual copies with Export backup before big changes.

7Passwords, keys & safety

  • Forgot a staff password? Any admin resets it: Settings → Team access → Reset password. With the cloud workspace, staff can also be re-invited.
  • Every admin locked out? On the sign-in screen: Administrator → Locked out? Use your recovery key. The key is single-use — generate a fresh one afterwards.
  • Wrong password too many times? After 5 attempts ARIA enforces a short wait that doubles each try (max 15 minutes). Just wait it out.
  • Working offline? Everything keeps working. With a cloud workspace, sign-in falls back to a secure local copy for up to 14 days without a connection.
  • Shared computer? Turn on auto sign-out: Settings → Team access → Auto sign-out.

8Troubleshooting

ProblemFix

"Couldn't read your saved data" at startupUse the on-screen Restore latest automatic backup button — a copy of the broken file is kept either way.
Invite code "isn't valid"Codes are single-use and expire in 7 days — ask the admin for a fresh join link.
App seems out of dateSettings → About → Check for updates now. Updates also install automatically within hours.
Agent replies fail with an errorCheck the Anthropic API key in Settings → AI & API and your internet connection.
Want a fresh startSettings → Data → Export backup first, then Wipe all data.
